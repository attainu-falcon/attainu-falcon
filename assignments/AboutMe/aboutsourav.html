About Sourav

Sourav is a 26 years old boy from Jitpur, Westbengal, India. He was very interested in Science and Mathematics since his childhood. He was very good at school life. He got 84% in the secondary examination. After passing the Secondary Examination, he was admitted to the science department. In the Higher Secondary examination he got 95 in Mathematics out 100 marks and got 91 in Physics. After passing his Higher Secondary Examination, he took admission in Santipur College for studying his favourite subject Mathematics.

Unfortunately, in the second year of his college life, he had to face a brain injury because of wrong treatment by a local doctor. He had been suffering for 5 years. At first, he was admitted in local hospital but his situation became worse. After that he was admitted to Apollo Gleneagles Hospitals, Kolkata. But the situation became more critical. After that his father decided to go to the CMC,Vellore for his treatment. But there also he was not recovered from his bad situation. Thereafter, his father and uncle took him to NIMHANS, Bangalore. At last the doctors of NIMHANS became succeed. They were able to give him a new life. But it took alsmost 5 years, from 2013 to 2018. He had to take medication till the March of 2019.

Although this 5 years of his life has caused much damage to his career, this obstacle was unable to stop him. when he became a little better, he began to teach students to buy his medicines. Because buying medicines had become a burden on his father as his father was a farmer. Along with private tuition, he was taking knowledge of technology and science from various online sources as his interest in technology and science was incomparable. Meanwhile, without having any knowledge of web development, he created his blog www.smartsourav.com and started writing about technology, education and finance.

He got Google India Challenge Scholarship in 2018. He got the opportunity to do an Android Developer Course from Udacity India by the help of Google. But he was not able to do his best as at that time he was under medication. After that he got an offer to work from Appen Global, Australia as an independent contractor. After getting this job, he gave up private tuition.

In April 2019, he applied for the Full Stack Web Development Course at AttainU and got the chance. AttainU is like a new hope for him.
